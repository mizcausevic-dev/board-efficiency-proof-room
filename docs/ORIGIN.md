# Origin

Board Efficiency Proof Room exists after the scorecards, trust-room, procurement-answer, RFP assembly, proof-gap, board memo, benchmark, scenario, allocation, priority, reallocation, preservation, and takeout layers.

The earlier tools show where to invest, what to freeze, and what to cut. This repo turns that stack into one proof surface so leadership can explain what landed, where leakage remains, which owners followed through, and which efficiency claims actually survive a board or diligence room.
